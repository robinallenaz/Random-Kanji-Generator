About Gengo Gateway

What is Gengo Gateway?

Gengo Gateway is an interactive Japanese language learning tool designed to help students master Kanji characters through an engaging and intuitive interface. With its unique wind-blown character background and modern glassmorphic design, it creates an immersive learning environment that makes studying Japanese both fun and effective.

How to Use

Simply click the "Generate Kanji" button to display a random Kanji character. Hover over or tap the card to reveal its meaning and readings. The tool is perfect for daily practice sessions and helps build familiarity with common Japanese characters.
